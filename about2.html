About Us

GGT Healthcare Limited is one of the leading providers of clinical laboratory and radiology services located in the heart of Alimosho Local Government Area, Lagos, Nigeria. Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art medical equipment and supported by highly competent healthcare professionals dedicated to quality service delivery.

Introduction

GGT Laboratory Services is a duly registered company with the Health Facilities Monitoring and Accreditation Agency (HEFAMAA), the regulatory body responsible for monitoring both private and public health facilities to ensure proper registration and accreditation across Lagos State.

At GGT, we offer top-notch diagnostic services to our esteemed clients. We combine vision, precision, advanced technology, and sound medical expertise to improve outcomes for both doctors and patients.

Our commitment to excellence forms the foundation for delivering efficient and effective therapy. We prioritize accuracy and speed, ensuring that diagnostic procedures are expedited and results are delivered promptly and reliably.

We are dedicated to providing highly efficient, evidence-based, and cutting-edge diagnostic solutions. Our scientific experts offer guidance on personalized diagnostic pathways and provide meaningful, accurate interpretation of results to support informed medical decisions.

Vision

To be a leading medical diagnostic center with focus on delivering quality affordable healthcare solutions, efficient and accurate testing with fast and reliable turnaround times.

Mission

We endeavor to make good quality healthcare services accessible to everyone in compliance to regulatory standards, ensure to the well-being of all and delighting stakeholders with our unique solutions.

Values

We strive to provide every client with high quality products and services. We deliver our promises in essence of the way we do business. We are progressive and innovative by constantly seeking to enhance patient care. We are committed to continuous improvement and professional development.
