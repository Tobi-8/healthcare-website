About Us

GGT Healthcare Limited is one of the leading providers of clinical laboratory and radiology services in the heart of Alimosho Local Government area, of Lagos Nigeria. Our facility is furnished with state of the art medical equipment as well as competent professional human resources.

GGT Laboratory Services is a registered company with Health Facilities Monitoring and Accreditation Agency (HEFAMAA) a body saddled with the responsibility of monitoring both private and public health facilities to ensure registration and in the near future, accreditation of all health facilities in Lagos State.

At GGT, we offer top-notch diagnostic services to our esteemed clients. We also combine vision, precision, technology and sound medical knowledge to improve outcomes for both doctors and patients. This lays the groundwork for providing efficient and effective therapy. And because we want you to get – and stay – well, we work hard to expedite diagnostic procedures and provide accurate timely results.

We are here to assist and offer you with highly efficient, evidence-based, and cutting-edge diagnostic technology. Our scientific experts offer advice on individual diagnostic pathways as well as meaningful and valid interpretation of results.

Vision

To be a leading medical diagnostic center with focus on delivering quality affordable healthcare solutions, efficient and accurate testing with fast and reliable turnaround times.

Mission

We endeavor to make good quality healthcare services accessible to everyone in compliance to regulatory standards, ensure to the well-being of all and delighting stakeholders with our unique solutions.

Values

We strive to provide every client with high quality products and services. We deliver our promises in essence of the way we do business. We are progressive and innovative by constantly seeking to enhance patient care. We are committed to continuous improvement and professional development.
