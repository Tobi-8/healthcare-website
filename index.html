What We Offer

Clinic (G.O.P.D)

Our General Outpatient Department provides quick medical consultations, routine check-ups, and primary care for a wide range of health concerns.

Dental Clinic

We provide professional dental care including routine check-ups, cleanings, and treatments to help maintain healthy teeth and a confident smile.

Eye Clinic

Our eye clinic offers comprehensive vision checks, diagnosis, and treatment to help maintain and protect your eye health.

Pharmacy Services

Our pharmacy provides safe, reliable access to prescribed medications and professional guidance to support your treatment and recovery.

Diagnostics

We offer accurate and timely diagnostic services, including lab tests and imaging, to help detect and monitor health conditions effectively.

Radiology(C.T/MRI)

Ultrasound

Non-invasive imaging to monitor and diagnose medical conditions, providing clear insights for accurate care.
